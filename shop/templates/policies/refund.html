Cancellation and Refund Policy

Last updated on Jun 13 2025

  • Cancellations can be requested within 2-3 days of placing the order.
  • Cancellation may not be possible if shipping has been initiated.
  • No cancellations are accepted for perishable items.
  • If products are damaged or defective, report within 2-3 days for review.
  • If you believe the product differs from what was shown, contact us within 2-3 days.
  • Refunds, if approved, will be processed within 3-4 days.
